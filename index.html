Our Profile

  1. At The Hatchery, we don’t just train, we change mindsets. The Hatchery works with organisations to shift mindsets, release latent potential and transform individuals, by providing top team training to reform individuals and groups. 
  2. Using the most effective training methodologies and processes, we work with individuals so that they may make the difference that makes the difference. We develop most of our training materials in-house. All gathered from university-based research, our global experience and the feedback received from our workshops and clients’ groups internationally. This explains why our programmes are considered unique, focused, relevant and current.
  3. Our consultants have worked with a corporate client base, which cuts across the UK, South Africa, Ghana and the USA. Clients include Cal Bank, Ghana Cocobod, Business Link, SFEDI, London Development Agency, Origin 8, AON, Norwich Union, Echo-House, Ogilvy & Mather, Macwest Ghana Water Company & Ecobank.
  4. The Hatchery’s Specialist Consultants conduct diagnostics with client teams to highlight gaps in skills, knowledge and abilities and curate bespoke programs for our clients.

Featured Service

BUSINESS PLANNING

The Hatchery’s unique approach out of all the incubators and accelerators in Ghana is its focus on individual capacity building and investment readiness for entrepreneurs in the specific industries that they work in serves. This includes a thorough revision of their business model while at the same time exposing them to strong local and global networks to give them the requisite tools and skills to access markets our entrepreneurs with access to our local networks.

ACCELERATOR APPROACH 

An experiential, participatory learning approach using the PROBE Approach coupled with elements of the CEFE methodology and the Lean Canvas is what we use in training social entrepreneurs (defined as “individuals who develop economically sustainable solutions to social problems”).

INCUBATOR APPROACH  

The Hatchery is well versed in running Business Plan Competitions using various channels including social media outlets and NBSSI Business Advisory Services for those outside Accra We will access the NBSSI platform which has over 60,000 social enterprises registered, the SE Ghana Network, GAWE and GADIA Platforms which also have thousands of women social entrepreneurs.
